# Innerspace

Innerspace is a web application designed for plural systems, allowing users to manage their systems, members, and fronting sessions. The application is built using PHP and utilizes a MySQL database for data storage.

For who don't know, a plural system is a group of individuals who share a common identity and often have distinct personalities or "members" within the system. Innerspace provides a platform for these systems to organize and manage their internal dynamics. Commonly used by people with Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID) or other forms of plurality, Innerspace aims to create a safe and supportive environment for users to connect and share their experiences.

## Features

- **System Management**: Users can create and manage their plural systems, including adding and removing members, and updating system information.
- **Member Profiles**: Each member within a system can have their own profile, allowing users to share information about themselves, their personalities, and their experiences.
- **Fronting Sessions**: Users can track fronting sessions, which are periods when a specific member is in control of the system. This feature allows users to log and manage their fronting experiences.
- **Community Interaction**: Innerspace provides a platform for users to tell others their current fronting status, fostering a sense of community and support among users.
- **Friendly Interface**: Innerspace is designed with a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to navigate and manage their systems and members.
